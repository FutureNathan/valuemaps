# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Value Maps exists to show that people can hold many hopes at once, and that we
don't have to sort each other into opposing camps. We bring that same spirit
here: everyone is welcome to participate, regardless of background or beliefs.

## Be excellent to each other

- Be kind, patient, and respectful — assume good faith.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and discuss them generously.
- No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or demeaning comments.
- No spam, and respect people's privacy.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any public space where you're representing the project.

## Reporting

If someone is behaving unacceptably, please report it by opening a GitHub issue
or by contacting the maintainer privately through their GitHub profile
([@FutureNathan](https://github.com/FutureNathan)). Reports will be reviewed and
handled in good faith and with discretion.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban anyone for behavior they deem
inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
